Timeline of Elon Musk's life so far

  • 1971: Born in Pretoria, South Africa to an engineer father and model mother
  • 1984: Codes a video game, Blastar, and sells it for $500
  • 1992: Pays rent in college by throwing house parties, shows early knack for business
  • 1995: Starts Zip2, an online version of the yellow pages, with brother with a $28,000 loan from father
  • 1999: Sells Zip2, his first business venture, for $22 million
  • 2000: His 2nd business venture, X.com, merges with Confinity to become PayPal
  • 2002:Starts SpaceX to build his own rockets after failing to by them from Russia
  • 2004: Invests in Tesla, works together with founders
  • 2006: Wins NASA contract to demo cargo delivery to ISS and contruct the Falcon-9 rocket
  • 2008: Narrowly avoids bankruptcy of SPaceX and Tesla
  • 2009: Unveils the Tesla Model S
  • 2013:Publishes the design of the Hyperloop high-speed rail, open sources the project to start-ups and unviersities
  • 2015: Lands first Falcon-9 rocket, disproving several rocketry professionals
  • 2017:Starts The Boring Company to dig tunnels that will implement a Hyperloop
  • 2018: Successfully launches the largest rocket since the Saturn-V
